Vizpediabeta

 
  • Type in a word followed by a space to get its image representation or symbol —also known as its pictogram— for the chosen language.
  • Some words may have multiple pictograms. Press on the displayed pictogram to see the other ones.
  • Other words may currently have no entry in the visual dictionary for the chosen language. In such case, no pictogram will be displayed. The symbols were originally aimed at Spanish speakers and its expansion to other languages is still work in progress.
  • As the text is changed, the address bar is updated accordingly. Using that address, you can share the page with the words you have already typed in. They will be added automatically once the page is loaded.
  • Use the erase button on the top right to clear all text and reset the address bar.
