  • Type in a word followed by a space, tab or enter to get its symbol —also known as its pictogram— for the chosen language.
  • Some words may have multiple pictograms. Press on the displayed pictogram to see the other ones.
  • Other words may have no entry in the visual dictionary for the chosen language. In that case, no pictogram will be displayed. The symbols were originally aimed at Spanish speakers and its expansion to other languages is still work in progress.
  • The page's address is updated as words are added. Using that address, you can share the page with the typed words included. They will be added automatically.
  • Use the erase button on the top right to clear all text and reset the address bar.
