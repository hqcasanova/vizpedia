How does it work?

  • Type in a term consisting of one or more words. To then get the corresponding symbol in the chosen language, follow the term with the tab or enter key. As terms may consist of multiple words, the space key does not trigger a symbol lookup.
  • The symbol is also known as its pictogram. Some terms may have multiple pictograms. Press on the displayed pictogram to see the other ones.
  • There may be terms with the wrong or no pictogram. Spanish speakers where the target of the original database creators and expansion to other languages is still work in progress.
  • The address bar is updated as terms are added or deleted. Using the expanded address, you can share the page with those terms and language setting included. The pictograms will be added automatically.
  • The erase button on the top right will clear all text and pictograms at once.
  • The page is printer-friendly: only the terms and their pictograms will be printed out.
